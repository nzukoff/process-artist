I am inspired by Aristid Lindenmayer's development of a formal language (L-systems) to model the behavior of cells of plants. It is a recursive system that leads to self similarilty and results in fractals and complex objects as generations continue. There is beauty and art in the digital and natural patterns that this set of rules creates.

Let's play with the rules!



Example pattern:

FF+[+F-F-F]-[-F+F+F]

  • F : Move Forward
  • + : Rotate Right
  • - : Rotate Left
  • [ : Start Line
  • ] : End Line

Insert your own pattern!
